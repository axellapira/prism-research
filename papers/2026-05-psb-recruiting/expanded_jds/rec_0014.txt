We are seeking a Product Designer based in Amsterdam to join a team building B2B SaaS products. The role involves owning end-to-end design work, from user research and information architecture through to wireframes, prototypes, and polished UI, in close collaboration with product managers and engineers. The designer will be responsible for translating complex business workflows into clear, usable interfaces and contributing to the evolution of the design system. Candidates should have prior experience designing B2B SaaS products and a portfolio demonstrating their approach to solving enterprise or workflow-driven design challenges. Familiarity with iterative product development, usability testing, and working alongside cross-functional stakeholders is expected. Strong communication skills and the ability to articulate design decisions to both technical and non-technical audiences are important for success in this role.
