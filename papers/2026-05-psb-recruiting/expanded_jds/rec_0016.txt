We are seeking an agricultural scientist based in Africa to contribute to research and applied work in food security, crop science, or sustainable farming. The successful candidate will design and conduct field and laboratory studies aimed at improving crop yields, resilience, and nutritional outcomes, while also evaluating sustainable farming practices suited to local agro-ecological conditions. Responsibilities include collaborating with smallholder farmers, government agencies, NGOs, and academic partners to translate research findings into practical interventions, as well as publishing results and contributing to policy discussions. The role involves analyzing data on soil health, water use, pest and disease pressure, and climate adaptation, and recommending evidence-based solutions to strengthen regional food systems. Candidates should have a background in agricultural science, agronomy, plant breeding, soil science, or a related discipline, with demonstrated experience working on issues relevant to African farming contexts. Familiarity with participatory research methods and cross-sector partnerships is expected.
